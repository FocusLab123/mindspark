Help & About

MindSpark is a focus and discipline companion. It is not a learning platform and it does not teach subjects directly.

You study outside the app — with your books, notes, videos, or classes. MindSpark helps you stay focused, consistent, and calm while you do that work.

Use MindSpark to:

  • Track focused study sessions
  • Build daily study streaks 🔥
  • Manage assignments and deadlines
  • Reduce stress with breathing resets
  • Train focus with short mental games

MindSpark does not replace studying. It supports the habit of showing up and staying disciplined.

How to Use MindSpark

1. Decide what you will study before opening the app.

2. Start a study timer and focus on your real materials.

3. When finished, your streak is saved automatically.

4. Use Mental Reset when stressed or distracted.

Privacy Policy

MindSpark respects your privacy. All study data and tasks are stored locally in your browser using local storage. No personal information is collected, shared, or sold.
